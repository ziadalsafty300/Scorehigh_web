Teaching Story

I started ScoreHigh Tutoring after going through the same process myself—scoring multiple 1500+ PSATs and a 36 in ACT English. I saw how confusing these exams can be, especially for international students, so I built a system that focuses on clarity and real improvement.

Teaching Philosophy

I don’t just teach content—I train students how to think. My approach focuses on identifying weaknesses, mastering patterns, and making the right decisions under pressure, which is what actually leads to score increases.

Personal Note

ScoreHigh was built by a student, for students. I know what it feels like to aim high and not know how to get there—and my goal is to give you the structure, confidence, and strategy to do exactly that.
